How it works

every musical playing right now.

Explore what’s on around the world without signing in — Broadway, the West End, tours everywhere. Sign in, and every show you’ve seen becomes a theatre passport you can share.

01

Explore — no sign-up

Open the map and see what’s playing around the world right now — zoom in and find which corners of the world have their lights on.

On the map

Every poster on the map is a show playing now

Markers are the musicals’ own posters; neighbouring ones gather into a numbered circle — that’s how many are playing around there. Tap a poster for its dates, theatre and ticket links.

Drag the month timeline at the bottom to any upcoming month to see what’s playing then; press ▶ to play month by month and watch tours move between cities.

One tap to tickets

Be in the room where it happens

Every show comes with a ticket link, and most have their official site too. We don’t sell tickets — we just point you to the right place.

Don’t leave memories all alone in the moonlight — stamp them in.

02

Keep every show you’ve seen

Once you sign in, your shows grow into a poster wall, a stamped passport and your own viewing stats. All automatic.

Your poster wall

What you’ve seen, hung on a wall

Every musical you add hangs its poster on the wall; tap the ♥ on a card to mark a favourite. Poster wall, passport, list — three views of the same collection.

Add a musical

Search the title, the rest fills itself in

Search in English or Chinese; theatre, city and poster fill in automatically. Add the date, rating, seat and notes — just the year is fine. Every show you log adds a stamp to your passport.

Make it yours

Your own poster, your own link

Give any show a custom poster URL — the artwork you remember it by, previewed instantly. Attach a link too, so that year’s programme or ticket page is always one tap away in its details.

Your theatregoing stats

How do you measure a year? In shows, cities and stamps

Most-seen musicals, cities and countries visited, how your theatregoing spreads across years and months — all tallied for you, showing at a glance what kind of theatregoer you are.

Share it

Your own personal page, shared in one click

The name you pick at sign-up is your personal URL; flip the switch in “Share” and the page is ready to send to friends. You decide whether price and seat are shown; your notes stay yours alone.

Signing in is simple

Sign in with Google, synced everywhere

We only read your name, email and avatar (the avatar appears only in your own signed-in menu, never on public pages) — we never post anything on your behalf. Your collection lives in your account, on every phone and laptop.
